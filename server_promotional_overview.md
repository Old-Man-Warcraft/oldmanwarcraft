# Welcome to Old Man Warcraft: WotLK, Reimagined for Adults

Let's be real—we all love Wrath of the Lich King. It's widely considered the golden era of WoW. But many of us just don't have the time to grind like we did back in 2008. We have jobs, kids, and real-life responsibilities now. 

That's exactly why we built **Old Man Warcraft**. We took the classic 3.3.5a client and packed it with modern quality-of-life updates, cutting-edge AI integration, incredibly flexible endgame content, and a unique individual progression system so you can actually enjoy the game on your own schedule.

Come check us out at [oldmanwarcraft.com](https://oldmanwarcraft.com)!

---

## 📈 True Individual Progression (PTE)
Experience the ultimate journey from Vanilla to Wrath of the Lich King at your own pace.
*   **Progressive Eras:** You don't just start in WotLK endgame. You progress through Vanilla tiers, then TBC, then WotLK individually. Your character experiences the authentic journey.
*   **Phased Content:** NPCs, drops, and world states react to *your* personal progression level.
*   **Relevant Old World:** Original Vanilla dungeons, raids, and Elite zones are restored to their former glory, perfectly tuned for your current progression tier.

## 🤖 A World That Actually Feels Alive
Hate playing on private servers where the world feels empty? So do we.
*   **Intelligent Playerbots:** Summon, command, and group up with smart companion bots. Only have two friends online? Fill the rest of your dungeon or raid with bots that know how to tank, heal, and do mechanics.
*   **Next-Gen AI Chat:** Powered by real LLMs! You can type and talk naturally to your bots and NPCs. Ask them for advice, coordinate strategies, or just chat. It completely changes the RPG experience!
*   **Active Auction House:** Our AH bot keeps the economy bustling 24/7. You can always find the mats you need or sell your auctions, even if you play at 3 AM.
*   **Global Cross-Faction Chat:** Stay connected with the entire server community no matter where you are or what faction you play.

## ⚔️ Raids & Dungeons on Your Terms
We've completely overhauled how group content works to make it endlessly fun and accessible.
*   **Flex Autobalance:** Want to run a 40-man Vanilla raid or 25-man WotLK raid but only have 5 people online? The server automatically scales raid and dungeon difficulty based on your exact group size and composition.
*   **Mythic+ & Challenge Modes:** We brought modern WoW's infinitely scaling dungeon difficulties, affixes, and timed runs into WotLK. Your favorite dungeons will never become obsolete.
*   **Instanced World Bosses:** Say goodbye to griefers and server lag. World bosses are moved into their own instances, allowing your group to progress and fight them in peace.

## ⏱️ Rates That Respect Your Time
We smoothed out the grind without completely ruining the feeling of earning your levels. 
*   **Experience & Reputation:** 1.5x (just enough of a bump to keep things moving).
*   **Weekend XP Boosts:** Because weekends are when most of us actually get to play! Enjoy bonus XP events every weekend.
*   **Economy & Drops:** 1x (Blizzlike) to keep the economy healthy and drops meaningful.

## 🛡️ PvP Without the Hassle
*   **Solo Queue 3v3s:** Don't have a dedicated arena team? Just queue up solo and the system will automatically match you with teammates of similar skill.
*   **1v1 Arenas:** Settle grudges or practice your dueling skills in a fully rated 1v1 bracket.
*   **City Sieges & Custom Titles:** Participate in massive dynamic faction battles in capital cities and earn custom PvP titles to show off your rank.

## 🎒 The Quality of Life You Deserve
We've added all the things you wish WotLK originally had:
*   **AoE Looting:** One click to loot an entire camp of mobs. Your wrists will thank you.
*   **Account-wide Achievements & Storage:** Your hard work on your main counts for your alts. The Goblin Bank system provides account-wide storage to easily pass gold and mats around.
*   **Guild Villages & Housing:** Buy, build, and upgrade your own custom guild headquarters in the open world, complete with portals, vendors, and exclusive perks.
*   **Transmog System:** Look cool while saving Azeroth. Swap your gear's appearance to anything you've collected.
*   **Robust Anticheat:** Play in a fair, closely-monitored environment free from hackers and exploiters.
