# Welcome to Old man warcraft: WotLK, But Better

Let's be real—we all love Wrath of the Lich King. It's the golden era of WoW. But a lot of us just don't have the time to grind like we did back in 2008. We have jobs, kids, and real-life responsibilities now. 

That's exactly why we built **Old man warcraft**. We took the classic 3.3.5a experience and packed it with modern quality-of-life updates, advanced AI, and incredibly flexible endgame content so you can actually enjoy the game on your own schedule.

Come check us out at [oldmanwarcraft.com](https://oldmanwarcraft.com)!

---

## ⏱️ Rates That Respect Your Time
We wanted to smooth out the grind without completely ruining the feeling of earning your levels. 
*   **Experience & Reputation:** 1.5x (just enough of a bump to keep things moving).
*   **Weekend XP Boosts:** Because weekends are when most of us actually get to play!
*   **Gold, Drops, & Honor:** 1x (Blizzlike) to keep the economy healthy and rewards meaningful.

## 🤖 A World That Actually Feels Alive
Hate playing on servers where the world feels empty? So do we.
*   **Intelligent Playerbots:** You can summon, command, and group up with smart companion bots. Only have three friends online? Fill the rest of your dungeon or raid with bots that actually know how to tank, heal, and DPS.
*   **Next-Gen AI Chat:** Thanks to our LLM integration, you can actually type and talk naturally to your bots and NPCs. It completely changes the RPG experience!
*   **Active Auction House:** Our AH bot keeps the economy bustling 24/7. You can always find what you need or sell your auctions, even if you play at 3 AM.

## ⚔️ Raids & Dungeons on Your Terms
We've completely overhauled how group content works to make it endlessly fun and accessible.
*   **Autobalance:** Want to run a 25-man raid but only have 7 people online? No problem. The server automatically scales raid and dungeon difficulty based on your exact group size.
*   **Mythic+ & Challenge Modes:** We brought modern WoW's infinitely scaling dungeon difficulties and timed runs into WotLK. Your dungeons will never become obsolete.
*   **Instanced World Bosses:** Say goodbye to griefers and server lag. World bosses are in their own instances now, so you can fight them in peace.

## 🛡️ PvP Without the Hassle
*   **Solo Queue 3v3s:** Don't have a dedicated arena team? Just queue up solo and the system will automatically match you with teammates.
*   **1v1 Arenas:** Settle your grudges or just practice your dueling skills in a rated 1v1 bracket.
*   **City Sieges & Custom Titles:** Participate in massive dynamic faction battles and earn custom PvP titles to show off your rank.

## 🎒 The Quality of Life You Deserve
We've added all the things you wish WotLK originally had:
*   **AoE Looting:** One click to loot an entire camp of mobs. You're welcome.
*   **Account-wide Achievements & Storage:** Your hard work on your main counts for your alts. Plus, our Goblin Bank gives you account-wide storage to easily pass gold and mats around.
*   **Guild Villages & Housing:** Buy, build, and upgrade your own custom guild headquarters, complete with portals and vendors.
*   **Transmog:** Look cool while saving Azeroth. Swap your gear's appearance to anything you've collected.
