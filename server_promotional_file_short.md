# Old Man Warcraft: WotLK, Reimagined for Adults

**Old Man Warcraft** is a custom 3.3.5a WoW private server built for adults who still love Wrath of the Lich King but no longer have time for the parts of MMO life that get in the way of fun. Whether you log in for 30 minutes or a full weekend, the server works around your schedule—not the other way around.

Come check us out at [oldmanwarcraft.com](https://oldmanwarcraft.com) or on [Discord](https://discord.gg/thecgn)!

---

## Individual Progression
Your character moves through Vanilla, TBC, and WotLK in sequence. Older raids, dungeons, and zones stay relevant and tuned to your era. Friends can progress at different speeds and still share the same world.

## A World That Feels Alive
- **Intelligent Playerbots** fill parties and raids on demand—they tank, heal, DPS, and grow alongside you
- **AI Chat via Old Man Assistant** — real LLM-powered bot and NPC conversations with server-aware knowledge
- **Active Auction House** bot and a **Goblin Bank** loan system (with real consequences for defaulting)
- **Global Cross-Faction Chat** keeps the community connected

## Raids & Dungeons on Your Terms
- **Flex Autobalance** scales content to your real group size
- **Mythic+ and Challenge Modes** bring timed, affix-based replayability to Wrath dungeons
- **Instanced World Bosses** — no griefing, no tag wars
- Small groups can bring bots and tackle any content on demand

## Solo & Small Group Friendly
No giant raid guild required. Solo players, couples, and small friend groups all have full access to dungeons, raids, and progression systems without rigid scheduling.

## Rates That Respect Your Time
- **1.5x XP and Reputation** — meaningful pace without trivializing the journey
- **Weekend XP Boosts** and a unique **1.5x Twitch Live Bonus** — link your account and earn while you stream
- Blizzlike drop rates so professions and loot still matter

## PvP Without the Hassle
- **Solo Queue 3v3** and **1v1 Arenas**
- **City Sieges** and dynamic faction conflict events
- Custom PvP titles and recognition

## The Nemesis System
PvE deaths create persistent revenge targets. The world remembers who beat you—promoting them into powerful foes with unique affixes and scaling power. Track rivals with the **NemesisTracker** addon, featuring a zone-aware map using real World Map textures. Hunt them down for unique revenge rewards.

## Quality of Life
- AoE Looting, Transmog, Account-Wide Achievements and Storage
- Guild Villages and Housing
- Robust Anticheat for competitive integrity
- Modern convenience without retail bloat
